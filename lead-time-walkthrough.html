The Aging-in-Place Home Walk-Through

Could this home work for aging‑in‑place?

A free walk-through guide for people thinking ahead about home, with plenty of lead time.

A home that fits at 62 isn't always the home that fits at 82. Stairs become more difficult. Bathrooms become tighter. The neighbourhood you chose for raising a family may not be the neighbourhood that supports you in later years.

This walk-through guide is a structured set of questions to help you notice what often goes unnoticed: whether a home, yours or one you're considering, could support independent living for the next twenty (or more!) years.
